Data handled by Canu

Canu processes singing practice data locally on your device. The app does not run its own backend and does not use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.

  • Microphone audio is analyzed on device to estimate pitch.
  • Optional recordings, scores, voice profile, reminders and settings are stored locally.
  • Recordings are shared only when you choose to use the iOS share sheet.

Retention and deletion

You can delete saved recordings in the app. Deleting the app removes local app data, subject to device or iCloud backups managed by you or Apple.

Apple, support and hosting

In-app purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple StoreKit. Support emails are processed to answer your request. This page is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may process technically necessary access data.
